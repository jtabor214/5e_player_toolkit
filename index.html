What's This?

Walking along the river bank, you notice a dilapidated old journal lying on the ground just off the main path. You pick it up and notice that it is a random collection of papers and handwritten notes, all of which seem to make up, “Boblin’s Adventurer's Guide.” Between the half-rotten leather covers you see that it’s full of information about the different kinds of beings that make up Faerun. Surely, there is something of value hidden inside this amalgamation of pages…
